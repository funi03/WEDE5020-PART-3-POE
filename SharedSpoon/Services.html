Our Services

School Feeding Program

We partner with local schools to provide breakfast or lunch to children. This improves concentration, attendance, and overall student well-being.

Community Soup Kitchen

At mobile or fixed locations, anyone in need can access a warm meal in a safe and welcoming space for social connection.

Food Parcel Distribution

We distribute weekly or monthly food parcels to low-income households. Parcels include staple items like rice, flour, canned goods, and fresh produce.

Food Recovery & Redistribution

We collect surplus food from farms, bakeries, and restaurants, reducing food waste while feeding more people in need.
